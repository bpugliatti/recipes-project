Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is just the best, freshest and simple dessert to make. No baking needed. It's very hard to fail on this one!

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 6 spoons of sugar
  • 250gr mascarpone
  • cocoa powder
  • cold coffee
  • Dry biscuits

Steps:

  1. Separate the red and the white of the eggs and mount them
  2. Add sugar and mascarpone to the red part
  3. Gently add the white to the cream, to keep it fluffy and airy
  4. Alternate cream and coffee bathed biscuits and leave it in the fridge for at least 6 hours. Put some cocoa powder on top before serving.
