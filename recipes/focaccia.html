Focaccia

Description

It’s hard to beat focaccia in the effort-to-reward category. If you are intimidated by bread baking, this is the recipe I suggest making first, both for its simplicity and flavor. After all, this focaccia bread recipe is adapted from my mother’s simple peasant bread recipe, a recipe that has removed the fear of the bread baking process for many.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Seasonings like rosemary or tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Whisk together the flour, salt, and yeast first
  2. Add the water and stir with the spatula
  3. Slick the dough with olive oil…
  4. … then cover the bowl preferably with a lid. or a cloth bowl cover. Stick the bowl in the fridge immediately; leave it there to rise for 12 to 18 hours (or for as long as three days).
